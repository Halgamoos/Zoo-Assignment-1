Peacock

Peacocks are also known as peafowls. Fun fact: these eye-catching birds are all males. They use their blue fan crested feathers to attract female mates. Their lifespan ranges from 10 to 25 years if out in the wild and up to half a century if held captive. Furthermore, they are the national animal of India. The most recognizable species of these peacocks include that from India and Sri Lanka(Pavo cristatus), the Javanese from Myannar and the Congo Peacock from the Democratic Republic of Congo.
