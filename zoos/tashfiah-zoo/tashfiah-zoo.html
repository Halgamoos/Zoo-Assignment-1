Peacock

Fun fact these eye catching birds are all males. They use their feathers to attact female mates. Their life span ranges from 10 to 25 years if out in the ild and up to half a century if held captive. Further, they are the national animal of India.

Penguin

Penguins are categorized as birds with special aquatic abilities and unable to fly like many other bird counterparts. They spend up to 75 percent of their life in the water. There are around 17 to 19 species of penguins and majority live in the Southern hemisphere!

Bengal Tiger

The Bengal Tiger native to the South Asian region are fierce and quite solitary creatures. Notable for their stripes they are unfortunatley only 2,500 left in the wild. They are also known to be highly accurate in killing their prey.

Giraffe

Typically native to the region of Africa, Giraffe's are the tallest mammals in the world with a herbivore diet. Fun fact they are able to stand within 30 minutes of birth and stand for majority of their days when awake as well as when they are asleep! They can live up to 25 years and extremely friendly creatures.
