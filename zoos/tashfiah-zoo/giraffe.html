Giraffe

Typically native to the region of Africa, Giraffe's are the tallest mammals in the world with a herbivore diet. Fun fact they are able to stand within 30 minutes of birth and stand for majority of their days when awake as well as when they are asleep! They can live up to 25 years and extremely friendly creatures. With the help of their long legs, they can reach up to speeds of 37 mph when sprinting. Each giraffe has its own set of brown marking patches so they can be told apart. Further, when fighting they often use their head as first line of defense.
