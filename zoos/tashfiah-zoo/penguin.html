Penguin

Penguins are categorized as birds with special aquatic abilities and unable to fly like many other bird counterparts. They spend up to 75 percent of their life in the water. There are around 17 to 19 species of penguins and majority live in the Southern hemisphere! With a region breakdown, penguins can be found include tiny blue penguins found in Australia and New Zealnd while emperor penguins are found in Antartica. Gentoo penguins are notable for their quick speed amongst other penguin counterparts reaching swimming speeds up to 36 km/h!
