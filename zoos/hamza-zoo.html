Lion

The lion is a large carnivorous mammal known for its majestic appearance and roaring vocalizations. Lions are often referred to as the "king of the jungle" and are known for their social behavior, living in prides.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their long trunks, which they use for various tasks, including eating, drinking, and communicating with other elephants.

Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest mammal in the world, with its long neck and distinct spotted coat. Giraffes are herbivores and use their height to reach leaves high up in trees for food.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest cat species and is known for its distinctive orange coat with black stripes. Tigers are solitary hunters and are often associated with strength and agility.
